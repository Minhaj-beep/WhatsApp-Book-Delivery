Privacy Policy

School Books & Stationery Ordering via WhatsApp  |  Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our school books and stationery ordering service, including through WhatsApp Business messaging.

1. Who We Are

We operate a school books and stationery ordering platform that allows parents, guardians, and schools to place and manage orders via WhatsApp Business messaging. We act as the data controller for the personal information you provide when using this service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you interact with us via WhatsApp or our platform:

  • Contact information: Your WhatsApp phone number and display name.
  • Order information: Details of books, stationery, or items ordered, quantities, and delivery preferences.
  • School information: The school name and code associated with your order.
  • Payment information: Payment status and transaction references (we do not store full card or bank details).
  • Message content: The text of messages you send to us through WhatsApp to process and fulfil your orders.
  • Delivery information: Shipping address and delivery status provided for order fulfilment.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your book and stationery orders.
  • Sending order confirmations, updates, and delivery notifications via WhatsApp.
  • Responding to your queries and customer support requests.
  • Coordinating with schools regarding order lists and delivery schedules.
  • Processing payments and maintaining transaction records.
  • Improving our ordering service and resolving technical issues.

4. WhatsApp Business Messaging

We use the WhatsApp Business Platform (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to communicate with you. When you message us on WhatsApp:

  • Your phone number and messages are processed through Meta's WhatsApp Business API.
  • Messages are subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy in addition to this policy.
  • We only send you messages related to your orders or in direct response to your enquiries.
  • We do not use your WhatsApp number for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only with:

  • Schools: Order details are shared with the relevant school to prepare and manage your child's booklist.
  • Delivery partners: Your name and delivery address are shared with logistics providers (such as Delhivery) to fulfil your shipment.
  • Payment processors: Transaction data is shared with payment gateways (such as Razorpay) to process payments securely.
  • Meta / WhatsApp: Message data is processed through the WhatsApp Business Platform as required for delivery of messages.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, including maintaining order records for accounting and legal compliance. WhatsApp message logs are retained for customer support and order dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our platform uses encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access to your data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you via WhatsApp.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for use by parents, guardians, and school administrators to place orders on behalf of students. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Order information relating to students (such as school name and booklist) is provided by their parent or guardian.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us via WhatsApp or through your school's designated ordering contact.

We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 30 days.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
