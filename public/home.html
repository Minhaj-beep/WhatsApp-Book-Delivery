The Process

From WhatsApp message to doorstep delivery

Our automated ordering flow guides parents through selecting items, choosing delivery, and paying — all within WhatsApp.

1

Parent messages us

A parent starts a conversation on our WhatsApp Business number. They share their school's unique 4-digit code to identify their school.

2

Select class & items

Our chatbot presents the correct booklist for their child's class. Parents select the books and stationery items they need.

3

Choose delivery

Parents choose home delivery (with address) or collection from school. The order total and delivery charge are confirmed.

4

Pay via Razorpay

A secure Razorpay payment link is shared in chat. Parents pay online — cards, UPI, wallets all accepted.

5

Order is packed

On payment confirmation, the order moves to fulfilment. Our team packs the books and stationery with care.

6

Shipped & tracked

Shipments are dispatched via Delhivery. Parents receive an AWB tracking number over WhatsApp and can follow delivery live.

For Schools

One platform for every school's booklist needs

We partner with schools to digitalise their annual booklist ordering process — removing the chaos of physical distribution and cash handling.

Unique school code

Each partner school gets a private 4-digit code shared only with their parents to access the correct booklist.

Class-level item lists

Books and stationery are configured per class so each parent sees only what is relevant for their child.

Flexible delivery

Parents can choose home delivery or bulk-ship to the school for parents to collect — whatever the school prefers.

School performance reports

See order volumes, revenue, and fulfilment rates broken down by school directly in the admin dashboard.
